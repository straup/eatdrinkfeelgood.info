Carciofi alla Romana
Billiam
vegetables
vegetarian 

I got this recipe from Bill. He made it for flatfishnight once, I think.

Aaron Cope
Ingredients
  • artichokes
  • 1 cup  water
  • 1/2 cup  olive oil
  • garlic
  • mint
Directions

  1. Clean chokes, including lots of leaves

  2. Skin the stem and cut off the top

  3. Put artichikes in a shallow pan with water, oil, salt and pepper, mint.

  4. Cook, covered, until soft (20-30 minutes)

Notes

You may also use parsley in the place of the mint.
