Graham Cracker Crust

Source

New York Times [ 1 ]

Requirements

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2  cup  graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4  cup  sugar
  • 1/3  cup  butter melted

Summary

butter, graham cracker crumbs, sugar

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees.

  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar and butter until combined.

  3. Press onto bottom and 1 inch up side of a 9-inch springform pan.

  4. Bake until set but not brown, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove from oven, and cool.
