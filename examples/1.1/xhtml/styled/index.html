eatdrinkfeelgood 1.1 as xhtml, styled

These documents use stylesheets that I've written for personal use and are provided only as examples.

You are welcome to use my stylesheets however please keep in mind that they have not been tested with anything except Mozilla. Fixes are welcome.

By default the eatdrinkfeelgood 1.1 to xhtml stylesheet generates plain vanilla XHTML and it is expected that users will develop stylesheets tailored to their specific needs.
